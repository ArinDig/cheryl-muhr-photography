About Cheryl

Hello! I'm Cheryl Muhr, a photographer based in the heart of Austin, Texas.

With a passion for capturing authentic moments and genuine emotions, I specialize in family and senior portraits that tell your unique story. My approach is relaxed and natural, focusing on creating a comfortable environment where your true personality shines through.

Whether it's documenting your family's growing years or celebrating your senior's milestone, I believe every session should be a memorable experience that results in timeless photographs you'll treasure for generations.

When I'm not behind the camera, you can find me exploring Austin's beautiful outdoor spaces, seeking new locations for photo sessions, and spending time with my own family.

Family Photography

Preserving your family's precious moments with timeless portraits

Family sessions are designed to capture the authentic connections and love within your family. Whether it's a casual outdoor session at one of Austin's beautiful parks or a more formal portrait, I'll work with you to create images that reflect your family's unique personality.

Senior Photography

Celebrating this exciting milestone with portraits that showcase your personality

Senior year is a time to celebrate who you are and all you've accomplished. Your senior session should be as unique as you are. We'll work together to choose locations and styles that reflect your interests, hobbies, and personality, creating images you'll be proud to share.
