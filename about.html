ABOUT

The greatest joys, the small details, the moments that stay with us forever. Photography has a way of holding on to the things we never want to forget. It shows more than how we look. It captures the connection, the laughter, and the meaning woven into our lives.

I'm Cheryl Muhr, an Austin-based portrait photographer specializing in luxury family and senior photography for Austin's finest families. With more than twenty years of experience, my work reflects an elevated, timeless style shaped by a love of beautiful light and thoughtful, intentional direction.

I photograph on location throughout Texas and the United States, creating portraits that feel elegant, honest, and enduring. Timelessness is at the core of my work, and every image is crafted with the goal of becoming a treasured part of your family's story.
